Open any game for full-speed play. Press ENTER for instructions, then ENTER again to start a scored run. The gallery plays previews at 30fps and pauses distant games. RECORD opens the selected game and turbo-renders an exact 60fps .webm; add &speed=1 to its URL for realtime capture.
